About Me

Greetings from Evanston. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. Ying Wu at Northwestern University.

I am interested in computer vision and robotics, especially for active visual perception. My former researches focus on stereo vision, 3D mapping, moving-object detection and map repair. And I also developed a huge interest in other real-world vision challenges, including data insufficiencies, data imbalance, and lifelong learning.
