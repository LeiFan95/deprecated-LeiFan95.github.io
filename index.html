About Me

Greetings from Evanston. My name is Lei Fan. I am currently a Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Ying Wu at Northwestern University.

I am interested in computer vision and robotics, especially for visual perception. My former researches focus on stereo vision, 3D mapping, moving-object detection and map repair. The publication is an appropriate timeline to describe my research history to obtain a static and complete outdoor map based on binocular cameras.
